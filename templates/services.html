Weight Loss

Weight loss at FitGuru is a holistic journey, combining personalized workouts, expert guidance, and nutrition advice to help you achieve sustainable results and feel your best.

Weight Gain

Weight gain at FitGuru focuses on building muscle and strength through tailored workouts, proper nutrition, and expert support, helping you achieve healthy and sustainable results.

Personal Training

FitGuru's personal training offers one-on-one sessions with expert trainers who create customized workout plans, provide motivation, and ensure you reach your fitness goals effectively and safely.

Group Training

FitGuru's group training offers energizing, team-oriented workouts led by expert trainers, designed to boost strength, endurance, and motivation in a supportive group environment.

Diet Plan

FitGuru’s diet plans are personalized to support your fitness goals, providing balanced, nutritious meals that fuel your body for optimal performance, weight loss, or muscle gain.

Yoga / Zumba

FitGuru’s yoga classes promote flexibility, mindfulness, and relaxation, while our Zumba sessions offer a fun, high-energy workout to boost cardio fitness and burn calories through dance.
